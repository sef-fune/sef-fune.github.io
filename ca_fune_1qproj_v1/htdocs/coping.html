Coping Mechanisms


Possible Coping Mechanisms

  • Making art
    Creating different types of artworks, whether it be using a traditional medium or made digitally, have become great ways for people to express themselves!
  • Exercising
    Staying physically fit has been shown to help people stay mentally fit!
  • Doing chores
    Focusing on a simple task at hand, such as sweeping the floor or washing the dishes, can help distance someone from their issues and help them cope better.
  • Playing games
    Playing games puts you in a fantasy world, where your only task is to enjoy the game!
  • Doing self care
    Examples of these are eating food that you love, watching your favorite shows and taking a long shower.
  • Reading
    Discover new characters that will help you laugh, cry, and all sorts of emotions!

Wait, but what's a coping mechanism?

A coping mechanism is an adaptation to environmental stress that is based on conscious or unconscious choice and that enhances control over behavior or gives psychological comfort.(dictionary.com, n.d.)

Important Note:

There are many more coping mechanisms for everyone to use, some of which may not work for you. Try to figure out what helps you best manage your mental health!
