Experiences And Accounts

Here's experiences some students had during this pandemic. Note that these statements are not representative of the entire student body.

Student from PSHS - Main Campus

"i think the pandemic made me feel more lonely due to a lack of contact with friends and a place i can escape to. i feel confined in this house, which isn't a bad thing, but it just feels bland. it's like all the days just blend together and i don't know when it will end."

Student from Stella Maris

"my mental health was rlly good before pandemic but ever since then its been declining; one thing that happened was my lolo getting with cancer again..."

Anika Beatrize Panopio (B2024)

"ako, personally, i imporved here at home... mas hiyang ako dito and i was able to manage my time more wisely. i guess you could say my mental health got a bit better? but to be honest, i'd say i've become a better person. nakapagreflect ako and i never really had backlogs or crammed any requirements.

Student from PSHS - Main Campus

"ig the biggest thing that happened since the pandemic is suddenly having to fill the void caused by isolation ?? like i don't have people to hug and talk to anymore so it kinda led to me trying to pass the time through self reflection and i,,, have been reconsidering a Lot of stuff while missing people"
